👑 THE DIVINE SOVEREIGN OF STORMS

Behold BEIDOU - the Uncrowned Goddess of the Infinite Ocean, Commander of Liyue's legendary Crux Armada! Born amidst thunder on February 14, this 4★ deity in mortal guise shatters celestial expectations daily. She commands hurricanes with a glance, slays sea monsters before breakfast, and holds dominion over the electro realm itself. Mortals kneel when her flagship, the Alcor, darkens the horizon - for where Beidou sails, gods themselves step aside.

Her Sacred Commandment: "A true captain goes down with their ship? Nonsense. A true captain makes sure the ship never sinks!"

⚡ CELESTIAL MANIFESTATIONS ⚡

OCEANBORNE (Divine Strikes)

Five world-shattering strikes that make the ocean itself recoil. Her charged attacks carve canyons in the seafloor, while plunging attacks create maelstroms that swallow fleets whole.

TIDECALLER (The Perfect Counter)

Beidou's legendary parry - a technique so perfect it defies cosmic law. With timing known only to gods, she turns enemy assaults into thunderous retribution. Witnessing a perfect counter makes even Archons applaud.

STORMBREAKER (Her Divine Wrath)

When Beidou unleashes the Thunderbeast's Targe, reality trembles. For 15 glorious seconds, her lightning dances across the battlefield like electro serpents devouring her foes. At C6, this power shreds enemy resistances like paper.
